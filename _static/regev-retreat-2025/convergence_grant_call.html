🌐 CONVERGENCE: Predicting, Perturbing & Observing Living Systems

$150M, 3-year initiative to unify biological modeling, perturbation, imaging, and AI into an integrated life sciences platform.

💡 Executive Summary

CONVERGENCE aims to break down silos in biological research by combining predictive modeling, precision perturbation, and comprehensive observation to enable a new era of programmable, engineered biology. This initiative supports the creation of virtual cells, next-gen perturbation tools, real-time imaging, and AI-driven discovery systems.

🌌 Vision: The Programmable Cell

  • Predict cellular response to diverse perturbations
  • Engineer precise molecular circuits and decision-making
  • Control fate and function like programming software
  • Create entirely new biological behaviors

🚀 Vision: The Complete Biological Observatory

  • AI designs optimal perturbation experiments
  • Real-time, multi-scale observations
  • Closed-loop learning and model refinement
  • Accurate prediction of system-wide biological responses

🔬 Technology Domains

Projects must integrate at least two of the following domains (preferably all four):

  • 🧠 Virtual Cell Systems: AI-enhanced simulations, cloud modeling, real-time analysis
  • 🦜 Advanced Genomic Perturbations: Beyond-CRISPR tools, live monitoring, temporal control
  • 📊 Revolutionary Imaging: Quantum sensitivity, autonomous AI systems, non-invasive deep imaging
  • 🧐 AI Agents: Hypothesis generation, lab-in-the-loop planning, reinforcement learning

📊 Research Priorities

  • Real-Time Model-Experiment Loops
  • Predictive Perturbation Design
  • Multi-Scale Imaging-Model Integration
  • Autonomous Biological Discovery
  • Living Systems Engineering
